Features which give you more control

Contextual Triggers
Proactively engage with visitors with customizable messages on different pages and based on agent availability.
Lead Collection
Collect lead info while agent is away so that they can be informed via mail whenever their query is resolved.
Identify Anonymous Traffic
Reveal detailed profile information of individual site visitors - Name, Company Name, No. of employees and more, so that your agents can prioritise support tickets.
Chatbots as Sidekicks
Liz, our intelligent AI-based support bot is your first line of defence and handles all redundant queries. Your agents can therefore focus only on queries that require human expertise.
Advanced Reporting
Powerful granular reporting to assess overall and individual performances. Make data-driven decisions to increase overall productivity.
Smart Actionable Messages
Supports all forms of rich messaging elements - lists, carousels, buttons etc. Your agents can drive the user to take the next logical action directly from chat.
