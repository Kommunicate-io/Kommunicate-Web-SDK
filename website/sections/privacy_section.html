We collect and process payment information from you when you purchase any of our Services. This information may include your credit card information, billing and mailing address, and other payment-related information. We process it using third party PCI-compliant service providers.

We collect the information about how you interact with our Service, This information may include your IP address, geographical location, browser type, referral source, length of visit, pages viewed and items clicked. Your website, app and other property visitors, potential and existing customers (Herein referred as End Users) may submit information such as personal information including names, email addresses and phone numbers.
