Frequently Asked Questions

What are seats?

A seat will represent an agent or bot you have added in Kommunicate dashboard. Every active bot and agent will make a seat. Suppose you have added 3 agents and 2 bots, the total number of seats would be 5. Please note that our FAQ bot - Liz, is not counted in the number of seats. It comes as a free add-on to Growth and higher plans.

How many agents can I add in a particular plan?

You can add as many agents in Kommunicate as you want. You will be charged on the basis of the number of agents you have.

Can we host data on our server?

Yes, as part of Enterprise plan we can deploy the solution at your servers and you will have complete control over the data.

Is there a limit on the numbers of chats and contacts?

You can have unlimited chats and contacts with each plan. You will be able to serve as many customers you want and there is no cap on the number of messages exchanged. Though, we expect you to adhere to our fair usage policy.

Are you GDPR compliant?

Yes, we are committed to your data privacy and security. Please review our privacy policy to know more. If you have any questions about data privacy and security, you can mail us at privacy@kommunicate.io.

How does the 30 days trial work?

You can signup for free to avail the benefits of the full-fledged support platform for 30 days. Credit card details are not required for the trial period. Post the 30 days period, you can upgrade to your preferred plan or else you will be automatically downgraded to the free forever plan. We will not terminate the services.

Is there any limit on the number of messages exchanged?

No, you can exchange as many messages as you want.

Yes, you can use Kommunicate with just bots. Please contact us here.
